Peer Review
Peer Review is an open source, diamond open acccess (free to access, free to publish) scientific and academic publishing platform. We hope to become a non-profit, funded by the community that uses it, and governed collaboratively by the scholars who publish through the site and the workers that build it. We're at an early stage (closed alpha), but there are a number of ways to get involved and help out!
